Find My Voice

Find My Voice is an app, designed by myself and a colleague, that assists those with non-verbal disorders and helps them communicate effectively. It has features including talk to text, customizable commands, and built-in icons and images for people who may not be able to read the text.

Find My Voice - Website

After creating the Find My Voice app, we decided to create a website that would further explain both the goals of the app and the development process. You can visit our website at findmyvoiceapp.com or click the image or header above!
